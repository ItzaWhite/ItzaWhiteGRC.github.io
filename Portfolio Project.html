Breaking Through the glass ceiling:
Embracing Opportunities

Allow me to introduce myselfâ€”I am Itzamara but you can call me Itza. I am an immigrant and an IT professional with a story shaped by timing and alignment. In 1999, my parents made the journey from Mexico to the US, and for most of my childhood, I lived as an undocumented individual. Dreaming big felt out of reach, given the lack of viable pathways for someone in my situation. So, I entered the workforce straight away. For six years, I immersed myself in the world of retail sales, where I acquired invaluable skills like customer service, negotiation, and teamwork. However, the pandemic and a company merger disrupted my environment, leading me to deeply reflect on my job, my future, and the kind of impact I wanted to have. Summoning my faith in myself, I took a leap and applied for a cybersecurity boot camp. Overcoming the tremendous mountain that is self-doubt and enduring a grueling journey, I completed the program, with a renewed passion for life and all it brings. Since then, I have actively pursued learning and honing new skills, driven by my unwavering commitment to self-improvement. I owe my current position to someone who took a chance on me and made space for me at the table. I acknowledge the hard work I put in, but it's important to recognize that the table isn't equally open to everyone. Consequently, my aspiration and plan are to pull up a chair for the next person, opening doors of opportunity. This year I suffered an ischemic stroke. This left me without the ability to speak and with a weak left side. It was a devastating blow, and in a flash I thought my journey of learning was over. However, I refused to give up. I worked hard in physical therapy and speech therapy, and I was eventually able to make great strides in my recovery. I want to help others overcome such speed bumps along their learning journey. I possess a hunger for learning and growth. Guided by my deep commitment to helping others succeed, I am constantly seeking ways to give back to the community. I proudly brand myself as a cybersecurity ambassador, striving to pave the way for future women of color in this field, ensuring their voices are heard and their presence felt. My goal is to make the cybersecurity industry more inclusive and diverse and uplifting others along the way. Ultimately, my aim is to reach a point in my career where I can empower and support others on their own journeys.
